## 👨‍💻 &nbsp;About Me

I'm a **2nd-year Computer Engineering student at Turkish-German University**, focused on closing the gap between academic theory and shipping real products. My core interest is **data engineering and backend systems**, and I learn best by building things end-to-end — from the database to the mobile UI.

- 🔭 &nbsp;Currently building **[Kâfi](https://www.kafiapp.com/)**, a mobile invoice & warranty tracking app (v1.0 is live).
- 🌱 &nbsp;Deepening my knowledge of **backend architecture, data pipelines, and AI-assisted development**.
- 🏆 &nbsp;1st place at a university-wide **vibe Coding Competition** with a full-stack team project.
- 🗣️ &nbsp;Turkish (native) · German (B2 – TestDaF) · English (B1).
- ⚡ &nbsp;I like turning messy real-world problems into clean, structured systems.

## 🚀 &nbsp;Projects

### 📱 &nbsp;[Kâfi](https://www.kafiapp.com/) — Invoice & Warranty Tracker `Live · v1.0`

A mobile-first SaaS that turns physical paperwork into an organized digital archive. Users photograph invoices and warranty documents, and the app extracts the data automatically, tracks warranty expiry dates, manages subscriptions (Netflix, Spotify, etc.), and exports everything to Excel or ZIP.

<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Flutter-02569B?style=flat-square&logo=flutter&logoColor=white" /> <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Supabase-3ECF8E?style=flat-square&logo=supabase&logoColor=white" /> <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Gemini_AI-8E75B2?style=flat-square&logo=googlegemini&logoColor=white" /> — *I built both the cross-platform app and its marketing website.*

### ⚽ &nbsp;[Maç Kolay (Match Easy)](https://github.com/Bilgisayar-Kavramlari/MacKolayWebsite) — Local Match Organizer `🏆 1st Place`

A full-stack platform that connects players to organize local football matches and tracks per-player Reliability Scores. Built with a team; won 1st place in a university-wide coding competition.

<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/React-000000?style=flat-square&logo=react&logoColor=61DAFB" /> <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Node.js-339933?style=flat-square&logo=node.js&logoColor=white" /> <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/TypeScript-3178C6?style=flat-square&logo=typescript&logoColor=white" />

### 💊 &nbsp;Medicine Tracker — Learning Sandbox

A medication-tracking app used as a hands-on sandbox to learn **local notifications, background scheduling, and data persistence** in mobile-to-backend sync.

<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Flutter-02569B?style=flat-square&logo=flutter&logoColor=white" />
